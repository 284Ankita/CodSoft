Free Checkups

Prioritizing your health is our priority. We're offering free checkups to help you stay on top of your well-being. These quick and convenient screenings can identify potential health concerns early on, allowing for prompt treatment and better outcomes. Don't wait until you're feeling unwell take advantage of this free opportunity to invest in your health!

24/7 ambulance

For emergencies that can't wait, our hospital provides a dedicated 24/7 ambulance service. Our highly trained paramedics and emergency medical technicians are equipped to handle a wide range of medical situations and are committed to providing prompt and compassionate care while transporting you to our facility. In the event of a medical emergency, don't hesitate to call 911 or your local emergency number for the fastest possible assistance.
